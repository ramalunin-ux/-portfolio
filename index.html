About

A creative director
who builds the production
behind the content.

I'm Rama, a video production lead and creative director specializing in performance content. I run productions end-to-end: strategy, scripting, talent coordination, and post-production.

I specialize in performance content for e-commerce, health and wellness, fashion, beauty, and food brands. From UGC-style ads to full brand campaigns, with the same obsession with hooks, pacing, and storytelling in every frame.

What sets me apart is the infrastructure behind the content. I independently build AI production pipelines that automate footage organization, team communication, casting, script assignment, and post-production handoffs. The kind of systems that let a small team produce at a scale that shouldn't be possible.

Beyond client work, I run a YouTube channel with over 120K subscribers. Proof that I don't just produce content, I know how to build an audience with it.
